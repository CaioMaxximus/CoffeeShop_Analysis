# ☕ [Coffee Shop Analysis](https://www.kaggle.com/code/caiomaxximus/coffe-store-sales-analysis)

## 📍 Overview
This project analyzes sales data from a coffee shop located in Vinnytsia, Ukraine, with the goal of understanding demand patterns and identifying key drivers of sales variability over time.

The notebook focuses on time series exploration, combining temporal patterns with contextual factors such as local events and holidays.
---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze sales behavior across different time scales  
- Identify patterns by hour, day of the week, and season  
- Understand the impact of local events and holidays on demand  
- Explore baseline models for sales prediction  

---

## 📊 Analysis Scope

### ⏰ Time-Based Patterns
- Hourly sales distribution  
- Peak vs off-peak periods  

### 📅 Weekly Trends
- Differences between weekdays and weekends  
- Recurring demand cycles  

### 🌦 Seasonal Effects
- Monthly and seasonal variations  
- Long-term trends and fluctuations  

### 🎉 Contextual Factors
- Incorporation of local holidays and events  
- Correlation between external factors and sales spikes  

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Python  
- Pandas, NumPy  
- Matplotlib / Seaborn  
- Time series analysis techniques  

---

## 📌 Key Takeaways
- Sales exhibit clear temporal patterns across multiple time scales  
- External events (e.g., holidays) significantly influence demand
